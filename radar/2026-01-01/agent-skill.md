In use since 2025 H2 as a practical application of [Context Engineering](/methods-and-patterns/context-engineering/). Prompts are decomposed into task-specific skills, and the LLM's reasoning is left to dynamically pick the right skill for the situation, instead of stuffing every instruction into one monolithic prompt.
