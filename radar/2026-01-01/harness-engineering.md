The next step on the Prompt Engineering → [Context Engineering](/methods-and-patterns/context-engineering/) → Harness Engineering progression, focused on designing the runtime scaffolding (tools, control loops, memory, safety rails) around an agent rather than the prompt or context alone. Mostly relevant when building bespoke agents, so worth studying the theory and patterns before committing.
