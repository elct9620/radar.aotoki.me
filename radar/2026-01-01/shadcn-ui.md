After more hands-on use, shadcn/ui has proven its copy-in component approach: we get [tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com/)-based primitives that stay fully owned and customizable in our own codebase, with no version-lock to an upstream library. Worth investing in for new front-end work where design control matters.
