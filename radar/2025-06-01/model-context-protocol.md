An open standard by Anthropic for connecting AI systems to data sources through a universal protocol. MCP enables secure, two-way connections between AI applications and external tools, replacing fragmented custom integrations. Major adoption by OpenAI (March 2025) and Google DeepMind signals industry standardization, with pre-built servers for popular systems like GitHub, Slack, and Postgres available.
